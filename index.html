ABOUT ME

I am a London-based location sound recordist and boom operator, providing sound recording services for film, documentary and commercial content.

I have worked with brands such as Tesco, Ford, Wix, Candy Kittens, UK and international film production companies, and empowering organisations such as Black Women Rising and Cornerstone VC.
